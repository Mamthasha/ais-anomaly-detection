
Dataset Overview
Total records: 6,949,544

Columns: ['MMSI', 'BaseDateTime', 'LAT', 'LON', 'SOG', 'COG', 'Heading', 'VesselName', 'IMO', 'CallSign', 'VesselType', 'Status', 'Length', 'Width', 'Draft', 'Cargo']

Date Range: 2017-01-10 to 2017-01-10

Geographic Coverage: LAT 0.36 to 74.50 | LON -171.10 to 147.94

Data Quality & Statistics
Unique Ships: 11,176

SOG Range: 0.0 to 102.3

COG Range: 0.0 to 360.0

Missing Values: High prevalence of missing values in auxiliary fields (e.g., IMO: 4,035,664, Draft: 4,775,742), handled during the preprocessing stage.

Data Provenance & Acknowledgement:
Source: MarineCadastre.gov (AIS Data for 2017).

Purpose: This dataset is utilized for the training and validation of the "AIS Anomaly Detection" framework. It is provided here as metadata to ensure transparency in model performance and methodology.

Note: Due to the large volume of raw AIS records, only summary statistics are provided. Please refer to the MarineCadastre Open Data Portal to access the full-scale original files.
